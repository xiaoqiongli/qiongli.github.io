Qiong Li (李琼)

I'm a postdoc in observational extragalactic astronomy at the University of Michigan recently. I'm now working with Professor Joel Bregman and Jiangtao Li. I obtained my Ph.D. in 2020 August at Peking University, Kavli Institute for Astronomy and Astrophysics. My research centers on the observational studies of the galaxy clusters, high redshift quasars/galaxies and large-scale structures in the distant universe via optical, IR, sub-mm, and radio multi-wavelength observations. I have substantial experience in describing galaxy (proto-)clusters, searching for Lya nebulae, and investigating their multiphase CGM in large-scale structures.
