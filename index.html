Welcome to Yali Shao's homepage!

My name is Yali Shao (邵亚莉). I am a fourth year astronomical PhD. candidate now in Peking University. I am working under the supervision of Ran Wang on ISM properties in host galaxies of high redshift (z ~ 6) QSOs and the co-evolution between QSOs and their host galaxies using Herschel, EVLA, and ALMA. I am also a one-year exchange-student in Pete V. Domenici Array Science Center (NRAO) working with Chris Carilli from October 2016.

I studied in Shandong University at Weihai before coming to Peking University. My major was space science and technology. Weihai is a very beatuful coastal city in China, you are welcome to enjoy the sunny beaches and fresh air there.

I come from Jining of Shandong Province which is the hometown of Confucius and Mencius and the birthplace of Confucius culture.
